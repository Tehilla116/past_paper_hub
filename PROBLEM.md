# Problem Statement — Past Paper Hub

## The Problem

Students at Zambia University of Technology lack a centralized, reliable way to access past examination papers. Currently, past papers are shared through informal channels — email, USB drives, or classroom groups — leading to inconsistent access, no central archive, and extra burden on lecturers.

## The Solution

Past Paper Hub is a full-stack web application that serves as a centralized past paper repository. Lecturers upload examination papers directly. Students browse, search, and download them through a structured interface organized by school, course, and year.

## Technology

React.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL
